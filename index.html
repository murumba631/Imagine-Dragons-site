Imagine Dragons

About the band

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band.
They are a four-man band with the lead singer as Dan Reynolds, lead guitarist as Wayne Sermon, bassist as Ben McKee and drummer as Daniel Platzman They are known for their songs like Radoactive, Believer, Thunder, Roots and Natural
But their first hit song was It's time followed by Night Visions in 2012
They have released albums like Evolve, Smoke + Mirrors and origins
